My Two Favorite Breeds of Dogs

Golden Retriever

Johannnes89 - Wikimedia Commons - CC BY-SA 4.0

The Golden Retriever is a dog breed that was originally bred to retrieve game when their owner went hunting. They were used for retrieving game because of their soft mouth which let the Golden Retriever retrieve animals without injuring them. Golden Retrievers are easy to train and have a love for water. Also Golden Retrievers have a thick coat of fur and shed large amounts. In conclusion, Golden Retrievers are good at retrieving game because of their soft mouth, they are easy to train, they like water, and they have a thick coat of fur.

The German Shepherd dog breed originated in 1899 in Germany. It is a medium to large size working dog that was used to herd sheep. However due to its intelligence, strength, obediance, and trainability they are used around the world for types of work like disability assistance, search-and-rescue, and military and police roles. In conclusion, the German Shepherd dog breed originated as a herding dog in Germany and has become due to their strength, obediance, and trainability a worker in disability assistance, search-and-rescue, and in the military and police.
